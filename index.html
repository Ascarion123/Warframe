Warframe

What is Warframe?

Warframe is an online MMO game where you fight as beings known as Tenno in warframes. A warframe is like a mech you operate to fight for the sake of the galaxy.

  • Warframe can be played with many different fighting techniques.
  • You can go with a heavy tank playstyle or a stealthy playstyle.
  • You can play with up to 4 players cooperatively!
